The Best Movies according to Liam

My top 3 movies of all time

Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels

I love this this british classic. Guy Ritchie's 1st release is gritty, funny and instantly quotable.

This is Spinal Tap

I don't know of a funnier film than This is Spinal Tap. I first saw this when I started my own band and fell in love with the characters, storyline and the music. For a comedy film, there are some absolute bangers in there. So many great quotes and of course, you have to do it in their accents.

Mystery Men

This is funny, silly and again very quotable. Absolutely in love with this film.
